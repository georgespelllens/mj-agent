# 05 — Mannequin Head Series (Symbolist Collage)

10 painted mannequin heads with different creature/element overlays, in the style of various Dada/Symbolist/Surrealist masters. Anchored by `wandering eye` and `symbolic nabis`.

**Style:** eclectic collage, period revival, dual color palette per variant
